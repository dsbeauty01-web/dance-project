💡 Tips for Parents

📷 Camera tips: For best detection, make sure your child is well-lit with good light from the front, and stands 4-6 feet from the camera so their full upper body is visible.
🎵 Headphones: If clap detection isn't working well, try having your child wear headphones — this prevents the music from interfering with the microphone.
⚙️ Difficulty: Open the settings (gear icon) to adjust difficulty. Easy mode gives kids more time to do each move — perfect for younger children!
